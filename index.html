About Me

My name is Jason Schultz and I'm a full-stack web developer and content creator. I gained experience in web development as part of the UCF Coding Bootcamp and am self taught in media production. I love enhancing my web development work with media production to create visually stunning websites and apps.

In my free time I enjoy watching sports, where you can find me rooting for Orlando City SC, the Tampa Bay Lightning, and the Green Bay Packers. I also enjoy homebrewing beer, hiking, and traveling.

My skill set covers a variety of areas. In web development, I'm proficient in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node.js, React.js, MySQL, and MongoDB. In media production, I'm experienced in using Final Cut Pro, GarageBand, and Adobe Lightroom.

OleOle

OleOle is a sports statistics web app focused on Major League Soccer. It allows users to find all of the most important information regarding their favorite team.

The front end was created with Bootstrap and Chart.JS. The back end was built with Node and Express. MySQL and Sequelize was used as the database and Firebase was utilized for authentication.

OrlandoEats

Orlando Eats helps users find the best restaurants in Orlando. Users can either search by cuisine type or browse one of the restaurant collections. Once a restaurant is selected, the address and a map is provided along with reviews and pictures of the restaurant.

This entirely front end app is created with Materialize CSS. It utilizes the Zomato and Google Maps APIs to provide the restaurant information to the user.
